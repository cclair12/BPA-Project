    • Bow Target Stand

    • 8ft 2x4 QTY 3
    • "L" Brackets with screws QTY 6
    • Bungie cords QTY 2
    • Archery Bag Target
    • Saw
    • Drill
    • Tape Measure
    • Wood Screws

    What you'll Do:

    Step 1: CUt two of the 2x4s in half, leaving you with 4 4ft boards. These will act as the feet and legs of your target stand.

    Step 2: Measure a 3ft section of the remaining 2x4 and cut, This will act as the cross member of your stand, where you will hang the target. Set this piece aside for now.

    Step 3: Lay one of the 4ft boards flat on your work surface. Measure the placement of the leg halfway down the foot, about 2 feet. Attach the leg to the foot using two "L" brackets on either side of the leg. Repeat these steps for the other side.

    Step 4: Take the 3ft board and lay it across the top of the two legs, being sure to make the edges flush. Secure in to place with 2 wood screws on either side. Reinforce with two "L" brackets on the inside corners.

    Step 5: Using the two Bungie Cords, attach your target bag to the top cross memeber of your stand. You're now ready to take aim!
